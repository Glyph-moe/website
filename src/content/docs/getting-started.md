# Getting Started

Welcome to Glyph! This guide covers everything you need to start reading.

## Adding Extensions

Glyph doesn't come with built-in novel sources. You add them through extensions, small plugins that connect the app to novel websites.

1. Open **Settings → Extensions**
2. Tap the **+** button
3. Enter a repository URL (you'll find these shared by the community)
4. Tap **Fetch**. The app loads the available sources
5. Tap **Install** to install the repository

Once installed, each source appears in the **Browse** tab. Tap a source to explore its novels.

## Browsing & Searching

### Browse

The **Browse** tab shows your installed sources as cards. Tap one to see its discover page: featured novels, popular titles, latest updates, and genres.

### Search

The **Search** tab lets you search across all your installed sources at once. Results are grouped by source. Tap a result to see the novel details.

### Continue Reading

If you've been reading something, the Browse tab shows a **Continue Reading** card at the top. Tap it to jump right back where you left off.

## Reading a Novel

Tap any novel to see its detail page: cover, author, description, chapter list. Tap a chapter to start reading.

### Reader Modes

The reader supports two modes:

- **Paginated (horizontal):** Tap the sides of the screen or swipe to turn pages. The page counter shows your position.
- **Scroll (vertical):** Scroll through the chapter like a web page. The next chapter loads automatically when you reach the bottom.

Switch between modes in **Reader Settings** (tap the center of the screen to show the chrome, then tap the menu → Theme & Settings).

### Reader Controls

Tap the center of the screen to show/hide the controls:

- **Top left**: Back button
- **Top right**: Chapter counter (e.g. "3 / 142")
- **Bottom left**: Progress bar
- **Bottom right**: Menu with:
  - Previous/Next chapter
  - Go to chapter (picker)
  - Bookmarks
  - Theme & Settings
  - Search in chapter

### Customizing the Reader

In Theme & Settings you can change:

- **Font & size:** System font or custom, adjustable size
- **Line spacing, letter spacing, word spacing**
- **Text alignment:** Left, center, justified
- **Horizontal padding**
- **Bold text** toggle
- **Theme:** White, Sepia, Gray, Slate, Dark, Black, or custom

### Search in Chapter

Open the menu → Search in Chapter. A search bar appears at the bottom. Type to highlight all matches in the text. Use the arrows to jump between matches. The counter shows your position (e.g. "3/15").

### Bookmarks

Mark your position by opening the menu → Add Bookmark. A small orange indicator appears on the left edge of the page. Open the menu → Bookmarks to see all bookmarks for the current novel, jump to any saved position, or add notes.

## Library

Tap the bookmark icon on a novel's detail page (or in the reader menu) to add it to your **Library**. The Library tab shows all your saved novels with reading progress.

You can organize novels into folders, sort by title or last read, and switch between thumbnail and list views.

## Downloads & Offline Reading

On a novel's detail page, tap the download icon in the toolbar:

- **Download All:** Downloads every chapter
- **Download Unread:** Downloads only chapters you haven't read

Downloaded chapters are available offline. The **Downloads** section in the Library tab shows your offline novels and storage usage.

## History

The **History** tab shows everything you've read, grouped by time (Today, Yesterday, This Week, etc.). Tap an entry to resume reading. Swipe to delete.

## Content Ratings

Go to **Settings → Filters & Ratings** to control what content you see:

- **Maximum Rating:** Choose from Everyone, Teen, Mature, or Adult. Novels above your rating are hidden from Browse, Search, and Discover.
- **Blur Mature/Adult Covers:** Blurs cover images for sensitive content, independent of the rating filter.

## iCloud Sync

Your library and reading progress sync automatically via iCloud. Any device signed into the same Apple ID sees the same data.

## Settings Overview

- **Sources:** Manage extensions and repositories
- **Customization:** App appearance and reader themes
- **Content:** Content rating filters and cover blur
- **Storage:** Clear cache, manage downloads
- **About:** iCloud status, version, developer tools, diagnostic report
